About us

GreenSwap is a student marketplace built for campus life.

We make it easier for students to buy and sell useful second-hand items nearby.

🎯

Made for students

GreenSwap focuses on the items students use most, from textbooks to dorm essentials.

🤝

Simple and trusted

Students can connect with other students in one clear, local platform.

🌱

Better for the planet

Buying and selling second-hand helps reduce waste and supports responsible consumption.

Our goal is to make second-hand the first choice for students.

GreenSwap gives students a clear way to save money, find useful items, and pass things on instead of throwing them away.

  • Buy affordable items near campus.
  • Sell unused items quickly and easily.
  • Support UN SDG 12 with everyday actions.
